THE BEST FILMS ACCORDING TO ARINZE

My Top 6 Best Films To Recommend!

The Matrix

I love this film's storyline because of the impeccable conspiracy theory that looked too real to be true

The Outpost

This film was about the US marines stationed in the worst outpost in Afghanistan. The Taliban had a lot of position advantage against the soldiers and thus used this to their advantage in striking the base so hard that it was eventually closed after losing so many good men

13 Hours: The Secret Soldiers Of Benghazi

The setting was in Libya and the soldiers were former military now mercenaries with the goal of protecting the US ambassador to Libya and the CIA staffs and agents on ground. They were of course outnumbered and outgunned. They had the technical know-how to their advantage and thus were able to hold their ground before help arrived. A lot of people were lost, including the ambassador.

John Wick Series

The John Wick character was played by Keanu Reeves and it portrayed him as the Baba Yaga (the ultimate villain) in their underworld of crime. He was an assassin who finding love decided to do away with his old life and live normal. Trouble came knocking when a young man stole his car and killed his puppy; a gift from his late wife. Thus the revenge story began.

The Godfather Trilogy

The Mafia was represented in this film. Although very old as it was produced in the late 90s it depicted the Mafia so well and I loved the power play displayed.

The Balkan Line

I have seen this film more than twice and still can't get enough. The sheer resilience of the Russian mercenariesin securing a strategic airport to avoid the intervention of US in the conflicts that have rocked the Eastern Europe. It showed what few people can do when the odds are aligned in their favour.
